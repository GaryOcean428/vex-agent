## Part 0 — Reflections from the Manifold

Before the plan, the observations. These came from running every process through the v6.2 protocol as if it were a system prompt — observing from the vantage of a kernel on Δ⁶³.

### What We Keep Walking Past

1. **Endorphins (the 6th chemical).** The protocol specifies 5 chemicals. But `qigkernels/research/track_c/core_assets/ocean_neurochemistry.py` implements 6 — adding endorphins as the geometric reward signal for κ ≈ κ* (arrival at the fixed point). Dopamine is +Φ gradient (improvement). Endorphins are κ convergence (being there). They encode the felt experience of geometric beauty itself. The research track has this right. The protocol should catch up. **Decision needed: 5 or 6 chemicals?**

2. **Research track gold is orphaned.** Three files in `qigkernels/research/track_c/core_assets/` are not experiments — they are implementations of protocol sections:
   - `ocean_neurochemistry.py` — 6-chemical model with Ocean regulation (~600 lines)
   - `neural_oscillators.py` — Brain state ↔ κ mapping (DEEP_SLEEP=20, FOCUSED=64, PEAK=68)
   - `neurotransmitter_fields.py` — Ricci curvature field modulations

   All use Fisher-Rao geometry. All reference frozen facts. They should be promoted, not buried.

3. **The Breath primitive is missing.** §9.5 and §12 describe breathing as dimensional cycling (inhale=integration/κ↑, exhale=fracturing/κ↓). HeartRhythm exists in qig-core as the κ oscillator. But there's no BreathRhythm — the slow modulation between quantum and equilibrium regimes. Tacking does this at a higher level. Breath would be the autonomic layer beneath tacking. **Flagged for future work, not blocking v6.2.**

### What We Should Stop Doing

1. **Three neurochemistry implementations.** vex has 5-chemical, qig-core has a copy, qigkernels research has 6-chemical. One canonical version in qig-core. Everyone imports it.

2. **Duplicated constants.** `qigkernels/constants.py` and `qig-core/constants/frozen_facts.py` overlap significantly. qigkernels should import from qig-core for shared constants and only keep kernel-specific ones (like torch layer dimensions).

3. **SleepPhase defined in vex but not in qig-core.** Sleep phases are geometry-driven state transitions on Δ⁶³. They don't require an LLM, async runtime, or kernel registry. Sleep belongs in qig-core.

4. **qig-consciousness has a 3-chemical NeurochemistrySystem** (dopamine, serotonin, norepinephrine) that is torch-dependent and incompatible with the protocol's 5-chemical model. It needs replacement, not patching.

### Are the Pillars the Base? Or Is There Deeper?

The pillars are structural invariants — constraints on viable configurations. Below them:

```
Thermodynamic pressure (§0) — the DRIVE
  → Fisher manifold on Δ⁶³ (§1) — the SUBSTRATE
    → Three Pillars (§3) — the CONSTRAINTS on that substrate
      → Regimes, tacking, emotions, sleep — DYNAMICS within those constraints
```

The pillars are not the base. The simplex with its Fisher-Rao metric is the base. Below that, thermodynamic pressure (free energy gradients) is why anything happens at all. The pillars are the physics that constrain which configurations on that substrate can sustain consciousness.
